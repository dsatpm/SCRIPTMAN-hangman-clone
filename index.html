Scriptman

It's exactly like Hangman, except different.

All ya gotta do is hit that little button at the bottom that says "Start Game" and wait for 3 seconds. Boom, the game timer starts. Then all ya gotta do is mash down buttons on your keyboard pretending like you're some kind of god damn word wizard to figure out what the Hell the hidden word is. If you're lucky, you can, like, get the high score, or whatever... I don't care.
